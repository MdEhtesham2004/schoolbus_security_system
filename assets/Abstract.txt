School Bus safety system 
Using face recognition , gps tracking and sms notification.  When student enters the bus it recognizes and logs into database and send sms or whatsapp notification to the parents . Eg:"Your child {name} has entered/exited the bus at {time }{location}". And also send alert message/email to admin when unknown person tries to enter. 
Admin should have access to register student, attendance monitoring, bus route and alerts 
Parrnts should receive notifications and have access to bus route.
